Our Brands

Eyewear
The Ray-Ban brand has continued to evolve and develop over the past 75 years to sit proudly amongst the worlds most iconic brands. A classic luxury brand that's not only at the fore front of technology but never forgets its roots.
Carrera was founded in 1956 in Austria by Wilhelm Anger as the sports eyewear maker Wilhelm Anger Werker inspired by the Carrera Panamericana auto race. Wilhelm Anger developed and patented the ‘Optyl’ material, which was a heat-hardened plastic weighing 20% less than acetate resulting in a reduction of allergic reactions.
Nike is an American multinational corporation that is engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of accessories, and services. It is the world's largest supplier of apparel.
Diesel is an Italian company, it sells all kinds of accessories. The company is known for its surreal advertising campaigns. Its style, in particular, is known for taking elements from rock-and-roll for its collections of chic yet casual items.
Safilo is an Italian company that designs, produces and distributes prescription frames, sunglasses, sports eyewear, ski goggles and helmets, and cycling helmets under its own five house brands and 32 licensed brands. As of 2012, it was the second largest optical frame and sunglasses company, after Luxottica.
Hugo Boss is a German luxury fashion house headquartered in Metzingen. Boss would eventually supply the wartime German government with uniforms for organizations such as the Hitler Youth and Waffen-SS, resulting in a large boost in sales.
Tommy Hilfiger is an American premium company which make appeals. The company was founded in 1985, and today is sold in department stores and over 2000 free-standing retail stores in 100 countries.
Often known for his risqua adverts and his provocative methods of marketing, the Tom Ford brand definitely has a seductive nature. The Tom Ford collection contains vintage inspired frames that remind us of Hollywood, statement bold chunky geek chic and exquisite craftsmanship.
Lenses
The innovative ZEISS EYEWEAR Architekt Kollektion line of eyewear was envisioned by architect and designer Elmar Gauggel, and made a reality by ZEISS EYEWEAR. It features eight frames, each distinct and striking.The Architekt Kollektion combines bold creativity and meticulous precision.
Varilux® progressive lenses provide sharp vision at every distance even in dimly lit conditions eliminating the blurriness and low-light issues of ordinary progressive lenses.
Essilor is the world leader in corrective lenses. Its international presence and ability to meet local specificities enables the brand to adapt to the needs of consumers across the world.
Corning Incorporated is an American multinational technology company that specializes in specialty glass, ceramics, and related materials and technologies including advanced optics, primarily for industrial and scientific applications. The company was named Corning Glass Works until 1989.
In 1992, Essilor launched Crizal: a lens that resists scratches, reflections and dirt. It developed a lens that lets in beneficial light and filters out harmful blue-violet and UV rays.
